AussieConnect Plus Decision Aid Tool

Loneliness affects over five million Australians, especially older adults, leading to significant health and economic challenges. This online decision aid tool is developed from a discrete choice experiment that identified older adults' preferences for various support programs designed to reduce loneliness in Australia. Utilizing the DCEâ€™s insights and estimates, the tool predicts the probability of uptake for different program packages, enabling personalized support services based on the key factors older adults consider when seeking social connection.

How to Use This Tool

Welcome to AussieConnect Plus! This tool is designed to help you assess the likelihood of program uptake aimed at reducing loneliness among older adults in Australia. Follow the steps below to get your predicted probability:

  1. Select your preferences from the drop-down menus provided.
  2. Click the "Calculate Probability" button to see your results.
  3. Review the predicted probability and your selected program package.
  4. Download your selected program package and Uptake Probability chart for future reference.
